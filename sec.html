Shounak Kulkarni


Hello, I'm Vibhali Rao from 11 Commerce and I am honoured to serve the role of USG Crisis Director for the Second Edition of Earth Ignite Summit which will be held in Indian High School, Oud Maitha in November 2023. My public speaking journey began in the 7th grade and although my first two MUNs were scary and confusing, I was determined to learn more and eager to win some accolades in the future. I went on to participate in many more MUNs and public speaking competitions like the First Edition of this years Earth Ignite Summit and also chaired in an MUN last year. I encourage you all to participate in this conference, not just for the university credits but also for the thrilling and fun experience that it offers. Events like these challenge you to speak confidently and research thoroughly and also provide you with the opportunity to show off your diplomacy skills, share your opinions on world affairs, roast other countries (while adhering to decorum, of course) and socialise amongst yourselves to have a memorable experience. I sincerely encourage all participants to speak up during the conference and to defend their allocated country/person and contribute in establishing peace in the world (although I will try my best to destroy that during Crisis).
